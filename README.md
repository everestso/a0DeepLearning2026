## Amy Lansky

**Website:** https://www.amylansky.com/

Amy Lansky graduated from the University of Rochester in 1977 with degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science and earned her Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University in 1983.

After conducting research in artificial intelligence at **SRI International** and **NASA Ames Research Center** in Silicon Valley, she left the field in 1998 to pursue her interest in homeopathic medicine. According to Lansky, this transition was inspired by what she describes as the miraculous cure of her son's autism through homeopathy.

Since then, her work has expanded to include topics such as:

- Consciousness
- Synchronicity
- Manifestation
- Alternative healing modalities
- Commentary on contemporary social and cultural changes

### Background Summary
- B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science, University of Rochester (1977)
- Ph.D. in Computer Science, Stanford University (1983)
- Former AI researcher at SRI International and NASA Ames Research Center
- Author and speaker on consciousness, healing, and related subjects
